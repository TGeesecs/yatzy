How to Play Yatzy

The goal is to get the highest score by rolling five dice to make specific combinations.

Gameplay:

  • Click Roll Dice to roll. You get 3 rolls per turn.
  • After your first roll, click any dice to keep them. They will glow orange.
  • Click Roll Dice again to re-roll only the dice you didn't keep.
  • After 3 rolls (or earlier), you must click a score category to lock in your score.
  • You can only score in each category once per game.

Scoring Rules:

  • Ones to Sixes: Points: Sum of only that number (e.g., 5-5-1-5-2 scores 15 for "Fives").
  • Three of a Kind: Three same dice. Points: Sum of all dice
  • Four of a Kind: Four same dice. Points: Sum of all dice.
  • Full House:Three of one kind and two of another. 25 points.
  • Small Straight: Four sequential dice(e.g., 1-2-3-4) 30 points
  • Large Straight: Five sequential dice(e.g., 1-2-3-4-5) 40 points
  • Chance: Any combination of the dice. Points: Sum of all five dice.
  • Yatzy: All five dice showing the same number. 50 points.
